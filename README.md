# Privacy Policy - DC Bots

**DC Enhancements** built the **DC Bots** app as a utility and management tool for Discord bots. This service is provided by **DC Enhancements** free of charge, with optional in-app purchases and advertisements, and is intended for use as is.

This page informs users about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

---

## What DC Bots Does

DC Bots is a Discord utility app that allows you to:
- Browse and manage Discord bots for your servers  
- Generate and customize bot invites  
- Manage bot permissions and OAuth scopes  
- View bot info (commands, permissions, stats, etc.)  
- Save favorite bots or custom bot setups  
- Get updates and notifications for bots you follow  
- Access premium features and remove ads via subscription  

**Important:** DC Bots interacts with Discord’s public API using your provided tokens or OAuth authorization. **We do not store, monitor, or intercept your Discord messages or tokens.**

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. **Information You Provide**

When using DC Bots, you may provide:
- **Discord OAuth Authorization Data** – used to list your servers or bots (not stored permanently)  
- **Bot Configurations** – custom names, permissions, and descriptions you set (stored locally)  
- **Favorites/Settings** – locally saved app preferences and shortcuts  
- **Subscription Information** – in-app purchase status handled by Google Play Billing  
- **Optional Account Linking** – if you choose to link your Discord or Google account for premium sync or cloud features  

### 2. **Information Collected Automatically**

DC Bots integrates third-party services that may automatically collect:
- Device information (model, OS version, identifiers)  
- Usage analytics (session time, screens viewed, interactions)  
- Crash reports and performance data  
- Ad interactions (impressions, clicks)  
- Location (city-level, for ads and analytics)  

Collected through:
- **Google Firebase Analytics**  
- **Google Firebase Crashlytics**  
- **Google Firebase Performance Monitoring**  
- **Google AdMob**

---

## How Your Data Is Used

### Data Stored Locally (On Your Device)
- **Bot configurations, favorites, and settings** are stored locally  
- **OAuth tokens** are stored securely (if saved) for session continuity  
- **History and preferences** are saved locally for your convenience  

**We do NOT have access to any locally stored data.**

### Data Transmitted to Discord
- When you manage bots or generate invites, data is sent directly to Discord’s servers  
- **We do NOT intercept, store, or log this data**  
- Transmission is always through HTTPS (secure)

### Data Shared with Third-Party Services

**Google Firebase:**
- Analytics and performance data to improve app quality  
- Crash logs (no personal info)  
- App metrics for optimization  

**Google AdMob:**
- Ad display, performance, and personalization  
- Uses cookies or ad identifiers to serve relevant ads  

**Google Play Billing:**
- Handles in-app purchases and subscriptions  
- No sensitive payment data is collected or stored by us  

**Discord OAuth:**
- Used for login and bot authorization  
- We access only minimal profile info (username, ID) necessary for app functionality

## Advertising

The app displays ads via **Google AdMob** to support development and maintenance.  
Ads may be **personalized** based on your device’s ad settings.

**To opt out of personalized ads:**
- Android: Settings > Google > Ads > *Opt out of Ads Personalization*  
- Or use the in-app consent dialog (GDPR/CCPA compliant)

Premium users can enjoy an **ad-free** experience.

---

## Data Security

We take your privacy and data security seriously:
- OAuth tokens (if saved) are stored using **EncryptedSharedPreferences** (AES-256 encryption)  
- All communication with Discord and Firebase is via **HTTPS**  
- Firebase security rules restrict unauthorized access  
- Optional **biometric authentication** (face/fingerprint) adds an extra layer of security  

However, no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## Data Retention and Deletion

### Local Data
- All bot configurations, favorites, and app settings are stored locally  
- Delete them anytime from app settings or by uninstalling the app  

### Cloud Data (If Applicable)
- If you sync or back up your data, it’s stored in Firebase under your account ID  
- You can request deletion anytime via the app or email  

### Third-Party Services
- Data handled by Google Firebase, AdMob, and Play Billing follows their respective retention policies  
- You can opt out of personalized ads anytime
